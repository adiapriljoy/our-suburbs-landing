Welcome to the beautiful Lockyer Valley

Affectionately known as Australia’s Salad Bowl, the Lockyer Valley blends diverse, thriving agriculture with the convenience of being less than two hours from Brisbane. This perfect balance of town and country living fuels the Lockyer Valley’s strong ongoing exciting growth.

All suburbs showing strong sustainable growth

We have highlighted selected suburbs across the Lockyer Valley to showcase why the whole region enjoys strong, sustainable growth. Producing around 40% of Queensland’s fresh produce, the Lockyer Valley plays a vital role in food security — a priority on both national and international agendas. This unique position not only underpins the region’s economic strength but also brings long-term confidence to its growth and future.

Lockyer Valley Health & Medical

The Laidley Hospital, a vital part of the West Moreton Health network, delivers essential healthcare, including emergency department, general medical services, and modern outpatient clinics. It is complimented by advanced medical centres specialising in dermatology, ophthalmology, and other services, along with regular visits from leading specialists.

Laidley

Laidley is a thriving country town with a warm community and rich farming heritage.

Just over an hour from Brisbane and the Gold Coast, it blends rural charm with city convenience.

The town offers schools, hospital, health services, shops, and vibrant weekend markets.

Housing ranges from character homes to modern builds and acreage, giving excellent value.

  • Median house price: $531,788
  • Annual growth: 17.4%
  • Rental yield (houses): 4.95%
  • Median weekly rent: $500

Gatton

Gatton is the heart of the Lockyer Valley, combining rural charm with modern convenience.

facilities.
