Welcome to the beautiful Lockyer Valley

The Lockyer Valley is a local government area in the West Moreton region of Southeast Queensland.

Affectionately known as Australia’s Salad Bowl, the Lockyer Vlley blends diverse, thriving agriculture with the convenience of being less than two hours from Brisbane.

This perfect balance of town and country living fuels the Lockyer Valley's strong ongoing exciting growth.
